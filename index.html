    About Me

    Passionate about mechanical engineering since childhood, I explored the intricate dance between curiosity and machinery. In high school, I turned this passion into a practical application by creating an IoT-based home automation system. Pursuing Mechanical Engineering at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, I became the youngest Captain and Lead driver in Team Endurance Racing, specializing in off-road vehicles.



    My internships at Defence Research and Development Organisation and CAD & CART enriched my practical knowledge. At Defence Research, I contributed to RFI tow-peg Additive Manufacturing, while at CAD & CART, I developed a windmill drivetrain with an Electronics Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT). These experiences deepened my understanding of practical applications in mechanical engineering.
